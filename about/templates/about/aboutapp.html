Where Was Our Company Founded?

Django Weather Services (DWS) was founded in 2021, by a group of university students looking to future proof themselves. They went about this by learning how to code in Python. Django, a high-level Python based framework, was developed around three core principles: ridiculously fast, reassuringly secure, and exceedingly scalable. In particular, this site uses OpenWeatherMap's 'OneCall' API, providing both current and historical weather information for our valued guests.
